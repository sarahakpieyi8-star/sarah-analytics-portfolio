# Sales and Profit Analytical Dashboard 2021-2024 📈

## Project Overview

Comprehensive Excel dashboard analyzing sales and profit performance across a 4-year period (2021-2024). This advanced analytical dashboard provides deep insights into profitability, regional performance, product categories, and identifies both opportunities and problem areas.

## Dashboard Features

✅ **Profitability Analysis** - Profit and loss tracking
✅ **Regional Performance** - Top and bottom performing states
✅ **Product & Sub-Category Analysis** - Detailed performance metrics
✅ **Yearly Trends** - Revenue patterns across 2021-2024
✅ **Loss Analysis** - Identifying problem areas and opportunities
✅ **Interactive Visualizations** - Comprehensive charts and insights

## Strategic Insights

### 💡 What's Working Well
✨ California, New York, and Washington are consistently profitable
✨ Technology category leads with 37% of revenue
✨ Binders and Copiers are strong profit generators
✨ Strong customer base of 728 customers
✨ High order volume (8,314 orders)

### ⚠️ Areas of Concern
🔴 Texas, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Illinois, Florida showing losses
🔴 Binders & Machines: High profit in some states, zero profit in others
🔴 2024 revenue declined 40% from 2023 peak
🔴 Loss sub-categories (Bookcases, Chairs, Tables) need attention
